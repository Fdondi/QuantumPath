Quantum Error Dungeon

QuantumPath is a fun game using quantum errors to make going around the quantum processor exciting.
Also made interesting by the peculiar layout of qbit on the processor -not all qbits are connected.
Send the kitten on a mission from Qbit to adjacent Qbit. They shoud all be zero, but... are they? If an error strikes, that can be the end of your journey!

Note the radomness of the errors has nothing to do with the useful randomness of superimposed states.
It's more of a tutorial monster you have to slay before anything interesting.
